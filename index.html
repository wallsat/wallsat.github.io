WalLSAT - a proxy solution that allows everyone to accept Bitcoin Lightning payments within minutes

WalLSAT


LSAT or Lightning Service Authentication Token is an open standard. It is used to enable payments through Bitcoin Lightning on the web. Lightning is a layer 2 scaling solution for Bitcoin that enables micropayments. Everything is done by directly opening a payments channel between two parties, so there is no long waiting for mion of transactions and little fees. Only in the case the other side is uncooperative you need to involve the Bitcoin network (similar to how business is usually conducted - only in case of trouble the legal system is involved).

To accept payments this way you would need to have a lightning node, open channels and take care about liquidity. Our solution allows you to offload all that trouble to us. Our servers act as a reverse-proxy sitting in front your existing infrastructure. Just two clicks and you can already accept payments. We accelerate access to your servers and your business.

Customers can pay truly anonymously, no need for them to sign-up or enter 12-digit secret numbers. Also if the price is just 1 cent or even satoshi that is no problem. You specify the price per URL or API endpoint and we take care of the rest. We also won't take a 4% cut like other payment providers.

WalLSAT can be used as a paywall solution to protect access to articles or other content or as a way to monetize your API.
